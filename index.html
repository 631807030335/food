Favorite food

Chongqing hot pot

Chongqing hotpot, also known as Maodu hotpot or spicy hotpot, is one of the traditional Chinese food styles. It originated from the Jialing River in Chongqing in the late Ming and early Qing Dynasties. It is also an extensive way of catering for Wharf boatman such as Chaotianmen. Its main raw materials are beef tripe, pig yellow throat, duck intestines, beef blood, etc

Chongqing Xiaomian

Chongqing Xiaomian, one of the four characteristics of Chongqing, belongs to the category of C hongqing pasta, originated in Chongqing, is one of the staple foods in Chongqing, especially breakfast. Chongqing Xiaomian is the simplest pasta in Chongqing..

Sichuan twice-cooked pork

Huiguorou is a traditional folk dish in Sichuan, also known as woguorou, huiguorou. It has a large number of eaters, so it has become a famous Sichuan dish with unique flavor. Sichuan people, who have been living abroad for a long time, visit relatives and friends in Sichuan. The first thing they think of is to eat huiguorou..

Sichuan pickle

Sichuan pickle, also known as pickled sauerkraut, is a traditional specialty of Sichuan cuisine. It tastes salty and sour, tastes crisp, has bright color, strong fragrance, appetizers and refreshing, sobers up and gets rid of greasiness. It is suitable for young and old people. It is a well-known food in Sichuan, China.

Wuhan dry noodles

Wuhan hot and dry noodles is a special snack in Wuhan city of Hubei Province, which belongs to Hubei cuisine; Noodles need to go through the process of boiling, supercooling and oiling, and then drenched with the sauce made of sesame paste, sesame oil, vinegar, chili oil and other spices. When eating, noodles are smooth, with tendons and strong sauce flavor, which greatly increases people's appetite..

Changsha stinky tofu

Changsha stinky tofu is a traditional characteristic snack in Changsha City, Hunan Province, which belongs to Hunan cuisine, which has been made by local people in Changsha as stinky dried fruit; It is dark and dark, and the outside coke is tender, fresh and spicy, crisp and not paste, tender and not greasy. It smells the odor at the beginning, and it smells attractive with a fine smell.
